Getting Mail as a Nomad

— how to get your mail with no fixed address —
• • • • •
Mail Forwarding Services
🏔️
Escapees Mail Service ~$18–35/mo
Nomad-run, Texas-based (no state income tax), scans mail so you can view it in an app and request forwarding on demand. Popular for full-timers who want a legit domicile address.
⚓
St. Brendan's Isle ~$15–30/mo
Florida-based, geared toward RVers and boaters. Same scan-and-choose setup, plus package acceptance for Amazon and freight.
📦
Traveling Mailbox / PostScan Mail ~$10–25/mo
Digital mailbox with locations all over the country — good if you want an address near where you actually roam instead of one fixed hub state.
⏰
Anytime Mailbox $4.99–$9.99+/mo
A virtual mailbox with 2,500+ real street addresses (not PO boxes) worldwide. Staff scan your mail so you view it in the app or browser, then choose to open & scan, forward, shred/recycle, or schedule a pickup — from anywhere with internet. Cheapest plans run $4.99–$5.99/mo, average plan is $9.99/mo. Price and included scans/forwards vary by location since each one is independently run, so compare a couple before picking your address.
💻
Online Mailing Systems (Virtual Mailbox Platforms) ~$10–40/mo
A whole category now — iPostal1, PostScan Mail, US Global Mail, VirtualPostMail, and others. All work the same basic way: real street address, staff scans + uploads mail to an app, you request forward/shred/pickup. Entry plans run $9.99–$15/mo for ~30 pieces; watch for per-page scan fees and per-piece forwarding charges ($5–$20+) once you're past the included amount.
Free / Low-Cost Options
🏤
USPS General Delivery Free
Address mail to "Your Name, General Delivery, City, State, ZIP" and pick it up with photo ID at that post office. Held 10–30 days depending on the branch — call ahead to confirm they still offer it.
🏠
Trusted Friend or Family Address Free
The simplest option if you have someone who can hold mail and text you photos of anything important, or batch-ship it when it piles up.
🅿️
UPS Store Mailbox ~$15–25/mo
Gives you a real street address (not a PO Box), which matters for banks, IDs, and package carriers that won't ship to a PO Box.
Package-Specific
🔐
Amazon Locker / Counter Free
Ship Amazon orders to a locker at a Whole Foods, pharmacy, or store near your current location — pick up with a code, no address needed at all.
🏭
UPS / FedEx General Package Pickup $5–$10/pkg
Ship to "Hold for Pickup" at any UPS Store or FedEx Office location near your route — show ID and grab it, no account or mailbox rental needed. Great one-off option when you just need a single package somewhere along your path.
🚚
Ship to Campground / Harvest Host Ask first
Some campgrounds and RV parks will hold a package if you call ahead and let them know it's coming — handy for time-sensitive gear on a longer stay.
Domicile tip: Your mail forwarding address can double as your legal domicile for registration, insurance, and taxes. South Dakota, Texas, and Florida are the most nomad-friendly states — no state income tax and mail services set up specifically for full-timers.
