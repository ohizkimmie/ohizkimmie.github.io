Unconventional Shower Spots

— for the nomads living life on the road —
• • • • •
🏋️
University Recreation Centers $20–$50/mo
Often feature clean showers, saunas, and steam rooms. Check for a community member day or monthly pass.
🏊
Public Pools & Aquatic Centers $3–$10/visit
Affordable daily drop-in fees for reliable, indoor shower access.
🏢
Community Centers & Local Gyms $10–$40/mo
Check local parks departments or budget gym chains like Planet Fitness for 24/7 access and reliable showers.
⛵
Marinas & Boat Ramps $5–$15/visit
Frequently have facilities for boaters that are accessible for a small fee.
🏖️
Public Beaches & State Parks $0–$10/entry
Best for warmer climates — keep a privacy tent handy for a discreet, refreshing rinse.
🎒
Hostels $5–$10/use
Ask the front desk about a "day rate" to use their facilities without booking a bed.
